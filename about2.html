Once Upon a Time...

My aunt and uncle gave me, as a pre-schooler (and too young to truly remember), a small spiral notebook called "School Days" with the hope that I would use it to document my K-12 years. Little did they know that book became my pride and joy and I guarded it like a treasure. Now, 15 years after high school graduation, it's starting to get torn around the edges and the cover, after holding on for dear life all these years, said its goodbye.

This site is just as much for me as it is for my parents, whom I thought would love to see my "school days" live on in a digital world. And one day, my future children can see the genesis of their mama as a little kindergartner all the way to a graduating high school senior.
